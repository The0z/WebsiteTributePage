Overview of Mr. Musk's Life:

  • 1995: After two days of beginning a Ph.D. of applied science he dropped out of school and pursued a career in business. He begins developing a web software company, Zip2, with his brother
  • 1999: Compaq acquires Zip2 for $340 million
  • 2000: X.com, an online bank founded by Elon is merged with Confinity.
  • October 2002: X.com, now known as Paypal is purchased by eBay for 1.5 billion dollars.
  • May 2002: Founds SpaceX, an aerospace manufacturer and space transport service
  • 2004: Musk joins Tesla Motors and later becomes it's CEO and product architect
  • 2015: Musk co-founded OpenAI, a non-profit research company that promotes friendly AI
  • December 2016: Founds The Boring Compay, an infrastructure and tunnel construction company focused on tunnels optimized for electric vehicles.
